Broken Pieces of Sunlight

This is my second book. I published this book when I was in the 11th grade.
Through this book I try to explore and properly explain what teenage dreams and emotions are like. The different perspectives that I hold on subjects such as nature, love, gender, etc.
This book is the result of a collective effort from all my family and frinds. I thank God for guiding me through my struggles and helping me bring this book to life.
